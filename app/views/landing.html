For Students

It’s no mystery that every student is different, they learn and react differently and expect to be treated like an individual. We are here to celebrate the differences in students by charting their individual learning trajectory – we do this by providing a data driven platform that engages the student. We not only believe in student outcomes, but student celebration !

The platform is set up in a ‘magical’ world and provides real-world rewards for students. Our primary research shows that kids respond better when learning is made fun in an environment they respond to. Real Awards provide the motivation for the student to return everyday with frequent treats rewarding growth in real life, not just virtual ‘badges’. A learner can even win a trip for their family to their favorite place by just learning.
Through deep prescriptive analytics, MLG uses data to place each student into the right level for each topic and then to adjust the level of skills, sub skills, hints & instruction so they retain what they have learnt and can succeed.

For Parents

Many students are challenged in trying to make a connection between lessons taught in class, a practice program and the high-stakes test. That’s why MLG a supplemental and analytics driven test readiness program aims to help through daily progress tracking with real time gap analysis that helps to create an individual learning path for a child. Parents can use this to understand their child’s growth and challenges and reinforce achievements as a child masters the skills assigned to them.

For Educators

In today’s day and age of mandatory high-stakes testing and continuous evaluation, educators need dynamic resources that aid them in real time skill gap analysis to accurately track a child’s learning trajectory.

Mylearninguru enables an educator to create customized learning paths for students and give real-time feedback in their class to accurately track the child’s progress. They can also upload their own content, change scope and sequence of the lessons and send custom assignments to specific students or groups to fill gaps.

MLG helps answers the most critical questions to educators

Which skills and standards have my students achieved mastery in?

How do I spend my time with my students in the most efficient way ?

What are their gaps? and Where do my students need to work?

What skills and standards are my students struggling with?

How can I build classroom groups for remedial, moderate and advanced learners?

Can I visually represent at-risk students, and what do they need?

Can I see progress by skill, sub-skill, national and state standards?
